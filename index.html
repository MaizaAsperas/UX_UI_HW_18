I’m Maiza Asperas, a Filipino Jr. UX/UI Designer and UX Researcher.
I find creating projects off of research backed results to be one of the most satisfying and rewarding jobs. I enjoy solving the question of “what-if?”, as well as solving user problems that you, or even I, have come face to face with in our everyday life. 
I’ve been involved with art almost all of my life as a hobby, and decided to one day finally get involved with design full time. It’s been a roller coaster that I know I won’t be getting off of anytime soon.
